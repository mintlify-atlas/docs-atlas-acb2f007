## Planning framework

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define your travel goals">
    Before researching destinations, decide what you want from the trip: relaxation, adventure, culture, cuisine, connection. This filters destination choices and shapes how you structure your time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Research and shortlist destinations">
    Use multiple sources — travel blogs, guidebooks, local forums, and AI tools — to build a picture of potential destinations. Consider season, visa requirements, budget, and how much time you have.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build a flexible itinerary">
    Create a day-by-day structure that leaves room for spontaneity. Overcrowded itineraries are a common mistake. Build in buffer days, especially around transit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Handle logistics early">
    Book flights and accommodation for the first few nights at minimum. Secure visas well in advance. Purchase travel insurance before departure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pack light and prepare">
    Lay out everything you intend to pack, then put half of it back. Download offline maps and translation tools. Share your itinerary with someone at home.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>The best travel memories rarely come from the planned highlights — they come from what happens in between. Build slack into your itinerary and say yes to unexpected opportunities.</Tip>
