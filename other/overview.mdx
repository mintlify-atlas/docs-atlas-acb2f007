## What's in this section

The Other section is a catch-all for projects that don't fit neatly into Business, Marketing, Creative, or Personal categories. It includes named initiative hubs (JJM, LALA, Toigo), lifecycle categories for work that is complete or deprecated, and family office and wealth planning resources.

## How to use this section

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Navigating completed work (Done)">
    The Done hub collects projects that have reached a natural conclusion. Use it to review past deliverables, extract reusable frameworks, or understand what was built and why decisions were made.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Understanding end-of-life projects (EOL)">
    The EOL hub preserves work from sunset initiatives. These projects are no longer active but remain valuable as reference material — particularly for understanding what was tried and what didn't fit the current direction.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Named initiative hubs (JJM, LALA, Toigo)">
    JJM, LALA, and Toigo are named project or relationship hubs. Each contains Genspark AI chat outputs specific to that initiative. Browse the individual hub pages to explore the captured knowledge.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Family office and wealth planning">
    The Family Office hub covers structures, strategies, and resources for wealth management and family business planning. It draws on Genspark sessions focused on financial structures, governance, and legacy planning.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
