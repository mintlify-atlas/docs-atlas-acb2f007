## Overview

FTK is the hub for special projects and cross-functional initiatives that don't fit neatly within a single business hub. It captures unique ventures, experimental concepts, and collaborative projects that span multiple areas of the business portfolio.

## Project types

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Experimental initiatives">
    Research and planning for concepts that are too early-stage for a dedicated hub. These sessions help validate or invalidate ideas before committing significant resources.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Integration projects">
    Projects that connect systems, platforms, or strategies across the business portfolio — for example, linking analytics from Insight Profit into FGS or Second Spring workflows.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Partnership development">
    Exploring strategic partnerships, affiliate relationships, and collaborative ventures that can accelerate growth across one or more business hubs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
